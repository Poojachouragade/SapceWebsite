All About Chandrayaan3!

Chandrayaan3, or Chandrayaan C, is the third lunar mission by India. The primary mission objectives include creating a soft landing spacecraft for a near-polar landing and performing an aerobrake maneuver to land softly.

About Chandrayaan

Chandrayaan 2, India's second lunar mission, is named after the Hindu moon god. The spacecraft was launched on July 22, 2019. The mission carries an orbiter, a lander, and a rover. The lander successfully deployed its rover, Vikram, on the surface of the Moon on September 7, 2019.

About ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the main organization responsible for India's space programme. It was established on 1969 and has played a significant role in the development of space technology in India. ISRO has launched various satellites, space probes, and other spacecrafts for both Indian and international space programs.
